partition layout must be custom and flash size must equal 16MB

MAKING LITTLEFS
mklittlefs-4.1.0-5-g68af3b9-generic-linux64/mklittlefs -c /home/minty/Arduino/sketch_feb16b/data/ -b 4096 -p 256 -s 0x300000 littlefs.bin
^^^^
replace command with your alias for littleFS
replace data folder location with your data folder location
the data folder's contents will be included in littleFS, limit is 3MB, but try to not exceed 2MB for good measure.

UPLOADING LITTLEFS
esptool --chip esp32s3 --port /dev/ttyACM0 --baud 460800 write_flash 0x610000 littlefs.bin
^^^^
replace command with your alias for esptool
replace port with your port that the esp is connected to (or remove the --port argument entirely to try with all ports)
